Mac's Estate Sale Services has been serving the Lansing, MI area for the last five years. Our careful team will handle your antiques, heirlooms, and personal items as they were our own. Whether you need a room or building cleaned out, or some items moved to storage or consignment, we will treat you with a level of care and professionalism that is hard to find anywhere else. That care extends to our community as well. Over the years, we have donated large amounts of food and clothing to local foodbanks and thrift stores.
